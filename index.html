Data

provides real-time Oil prices and rig counts. Interact with the pie chart diagrams by rotating tem individually or tapping on a section within the piechart

Oil & Gas News

scroll up or down to preview news articles, tap any item and youll be redirected to the corresponding article via a safari web view

Unit Conversion

Toggle the direction of unit conversion by tapping the green arrow in the middle of the screen. Toggle both picker views to select the desired unit ceversion. Input the initial value into the correct text field (depends on which direction the green arrow points), tap convert to execute the conversion.
